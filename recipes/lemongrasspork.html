Vietnamese Lemongrass Pork

Description

This is a classic Vietnamese dish and one of my favorites: Chargrilled pork that's marinated with lots of lemongrass and an explosion of umami

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Lemongrass
  • Limes
  • Sugar
  • Fish Sauce
  • Maggi Seasoning or Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Optional: Thai chili peppers

Steps

    Marinate the pork with all of the ingredients for at least 1 hour Cook on high heat Serve with rice
